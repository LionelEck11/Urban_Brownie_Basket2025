Urban Brownie Basket is a charming café located in San Ignacio, Cayo District, Belize. They offer a delightful selection of coffee, milkshakes, pastries, and books, making it a perfect spot to relax and enjoy a treat. The café is open every day, with varying hours throughout the week.
